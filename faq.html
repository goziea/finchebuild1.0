Investors

  • What is Finche?
    Finche is an online investment platform for renewable energy projects powered by blockchain technology
  • How does Finche work?
    Project originators list their renewable energy projects on Finche and interested investors distribute their token across projects they’re interested in investing in
  • What am I investing in?
    Our offerings currently concentrate on renewable energy projects and target annual yields between 8-20%.
  • Who are the typical Finche project originators?
    Small to medium scale clean energy companies that lack the funds to finance projects and are interested in obtaining medium to long term finance at reasonable interest rates
  • How are investments structured?
    After an offering closes on Finche, the investor capital goes to a newly-formed Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV). The SPV is a company created with the sole purpose of keeping your investment secure, regardless of the originator. The ownership of this vehicle is split among all investors in the deal or offering at a basis corresponding to your contribution to the deal. Similarly, when the borrower starts paying interest it is to the investors on a pro-rata basis on an agreed payment frequency. For example, if the borrower is raising $500,000 and you invest $5,000 in the offering, you will own 1% of the SPV and the underlying loan. If the loan pays 10% interest per year, you will receive $500 in interest for each year that the loan is outstanding.
  • How much can i invest?
    We have a minimum investment requirement of $3000
  • How do I make my first investment?
    To make your first investment, simply login to the Finche platform, explore projects available for investment and then click the “Invest” button

  • How long are the loans for and what are the interest rates likes?
